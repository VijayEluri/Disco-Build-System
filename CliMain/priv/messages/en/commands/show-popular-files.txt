Show all the files used in the build system, ordered by how often they are 
accessed by build tasks. That is, the most frequently accessed files will
appear at the top of the report, and the least frequently at the bottom.
Command-specific options can be used to filter the output.
		
Command-specific options are:

#include options/file-show-pkgs.txt

#include options/show-roots.txt

#include options/file-filter.txt
